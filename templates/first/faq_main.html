Insurance FAQs:-



Ans: We advise you to secure your travel insurance at least one week before departing from your country. Travel insurance is obligatory to trek in himalayas of Nepal. Your insurance should be comprehensive including helicopter rescue. These days different insurance policies are readily available through many airfare booking agents. We also inform you that Nepalese insurance companies do not have travel insurance policy for foreign nationals. We also request you to submit the copies of all legal insurance documents to our office before travelling with us.
